About The Hemraj Enterprises

Leading Manufacturer of Premium Eco-Friendly Bags

Our Story

Building a Sustainable Future, One Bag at a Time

We are a trusted manufacturer specializing in high-quality, eco-friendly bags including non-woven, PP, paper, and jute bags. With years of experience and commitment to sustainability, we provide custom solutions for businesses across India and worldwide.

Our mission is to reduce plastic waste by providing affordable, durable, and stylish alternatives that businesses and consumers love.
